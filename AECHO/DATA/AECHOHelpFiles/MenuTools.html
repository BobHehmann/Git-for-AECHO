The Tools top-level menu item contains three sub-menu choices:
  • Clear Markers
  • Couplers (Encode/Decode)
  • Trace a Sample
The Tools menu item and its sub-menu choices Clear Markers and Couplers (Encode/Decode) are always enabled and available, whereas Trace a Sample is only enabled while an ODF is loaded. All four items have "Alt"-key accelerators, but none have "shortcuts".

Clear Markers
Resets the four Marker Buttons so they do not point to any location in the ODF.

Click here to learn how to use Markers to bookmark locations in the ODF.

Couplers (Encode/Decode)
In an independent window, opens the Couplers form that performs bi-directional translation between Coupler-Codes and their functional descriptions. This form can remain open independent of activities in other AECHO forms, as it has no context specific to any particular ODF. The Couplers form can be closed whenever desired by clicking on the X in the form's upper-right corner; it will also be automatically closed when AECHO terminates.

Click here to learn how to user the Coupler function.

Trace a Sample
Opens the Sample Trace form in an independent window. This form allows the user to manually enter the SampleID for a (sound) Sample Record. Upon clicking on the form's Trace button, the desired Sample is located and expanded, listing all its possible Fields, both those present in the ODF and those not present. Sample Record Fields that are not contained in the ODF, or appear in the ODF but have a null value, are listed alongside their default values that Hauptwerk implicitly fills in. Trace then searches for Records from other Sections that are linked to this particular Sample, and if found, displays them in the same expanded format as the Sample Record. The Sample Trace form can remain open while other AECHO forms are performing activities, but is closed down when AECHO terminates, or when the user closes the loaded ODF, as proper operation of Trace is tied to an ODF. This form can also be closed by clicking its menu > Exit item, or on the X located in its upper-right corner. This menu item is closely related to the Trace Sample button, which is made visible whenever a traceable Sample Record is on display in the XML Row/Record Area.

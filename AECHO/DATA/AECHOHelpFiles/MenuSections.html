AECHO V2 Help - Menu > Sections (1) 1-22 & Sections (2) 34-44

<Return to Help's Home Page>

These two menu items provide direct navigation by Section Name to any individual Section of a well-formed ODF. There are 44 distinctly named Sections in an ODF: for convenience, these are divided into two groups of 22 each. Each of these two menu choices presents its 22 Sections as a drop-down, sub-menu list. Selecting a Section from one of these two lists navigates the ODF Display Area to the top of that Section.

These two top-level menu choices are only enabled when an ODF is loaded. When enabled, all of their sub-menu choices are also enabled. The two top-level choices have "Alt"-key accelerators, but no "shortcuts": their sub-menus have neither Alt-key accelerators nor shortcuts.

Navigating to a Section by using one of these menu choices performs the following actions:
  • Positions to the top of the Section, displaying the Section's Title i.e., its Start-Tag, at the top of the ODF Display Area. These Section Titles are emphasized by AECHO by increasing their font-size over the size of the body text, and presenting the Tag in Bold/Blue Font. Note that this emphasis is an artifact of AECHO's display, and does not exist in the underlying ODF file.
  • The Cursor is placed just before the opening "<" character of the Section Title.
  • The Descriptive Text Area is loaded with the Section's descriptive content.
  • The Status-Bar's Row Type: field displays the text "Sec Start-Tag", identifying the classification of this Row in the ODF: a Section's Start-Tag i.e., its Title.
  • If the Section contains Row/Record content (XML Elements bounded by "<o>...</o>" Tags), the first such Row is loaded into the XML Row/Record Area, and its Fields are located and displayed in the Tags Panel. Note that the first Row from the "_General" Section is not loaded or analyzed in this way, as it is organized differently from the other 43 Sections, using expanded, self-explaining Tags for its Fields.
  • The Data Panel Area is updated with the correct information for this position in the ODF: Caret, Line, Row, and Section.
The Section sub-menu choices available within each top-level menu item are:

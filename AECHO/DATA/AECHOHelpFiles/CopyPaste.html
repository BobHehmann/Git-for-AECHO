AECHO V2 Help - Copying and Pasting

<Return to Help's Home Page>

All four text-aware areas of AECHO's main form allow text selection at all times they contain content, highlighting the selection as it is made. Single, double, and in some case, triple mouse-clicks can all result in the selection of text (see Managing the Mouse for the exact behaviors). Also, all text-aware areas support the standard text-selection technique of "click-and-drag" with the mouse, allowing arbitrary regions of contiguous text to be selected.

Once selected, text may be freely copied using the keyboard's Ctrl+C combination, copying the selected text, including any formatting, into the system's clipboard. From there, it may be pasted into any text-aware program that allows text-pasting, following the rules of that program. If the program understands Rich Text Formatting (RTF), the pasted content's formatting will be retained; if not, just the textual content will be pasted.

Pasting into most AECHO text-areas is allowed by using the keyboard's Ctrl+V combination, but the rules differ slightly by area. When target-area content is already selected when the paste is issued, that content is deleted and replaced with the clipboard's content. If nothing is presently selected in the target area when pasting, then the clipboard's content is inserted into the target area at the Cursor/Caret's position. This behavior is the same as found in most editing programs.

AECHO does not distinguish based on the origin of content to be pasted: it can come from within AECHO, or from any other program on the system. In all cases, a paste operation in AECHO is just taking its content from the system's clipboard, without caring how that content got there. In most cases, even Images can be successfully pasted into AECHO content.

ODF Display Area
  • Copying of arbitrary contiguous text is allowed; may include formatting; may include any type of RTF content
  • Must have ODF Editing Enabled to pasting to be allowed; otherwise this area is read-only
  • Honors all RTF-compliant formatting, though formatting is not retained in an ODF file when the ODF is saved
  • Honors multi-line pastes
  • Accepts Image Content, but this is not recommended, as embedded images are not permissible in a well-formed ODF

XML Row/Record Area
  • Copying of arbitrary contiguous text is allowed; text in this area is not formatted
  • This area is read-only, pasting is not allowed

Search Area
  • Copying of text is allowed; this area is single line; text in this area is not formatted
  • Pasting is always allowed into this area
  • Embedded formatting is stripped, as this area does not support formatted text
  • Only the first line of multi-line content is pasted
  • Images cannot be pasted into this area, it is text-only

Descriptive Text Area
  • Copying of arbitrary contiguous text is allowed; may include formatting; may include any type of RTF content
  • Pasting is always allowed into this area, though content cannot be saved back to the Help Text; alterations to Section descriptions can be saved with all formatting and images intact
  • Honors all RTF-compliant formatting, and formatting is retained when saved
  • Honors multi-line pastes
  • Freely accepts Image Content; images are retained when the area is saved

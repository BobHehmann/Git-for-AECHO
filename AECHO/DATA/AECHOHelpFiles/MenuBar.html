The Main Menu-Bar is an operating-system standard menu system. Under Windows, the menu choices all have "Alt"-key accelerators, as do all sub-menus, except for the Section selectors underneath the two Sections menus. Alt-key accelerators are enabled by depressing the keyboards "Alt" key once, at which time all the menu items will have a single character appear underlined: typing that single character will open/engage that menu or sub-menu choice. Once a menu selection has been made, or the menu-bar loses focus, the underlines are hidden until the next use of the Alt key. In this documentation, menu/sub-menu choices are always shown with their Alt-key accelerators underlined.

Sub-menu choices that are common across a wide range of applications, such as open, close, save, exit, about, and help, also all have standard shortcuts, such as Ctrl+O to open a file, or Alt-F4 to exit.
